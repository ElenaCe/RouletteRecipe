Our Team

We are a team of four coding newbies who wanted to create an app to help make your life easier.
We’ve used a combination of our skills and APIs to build a simple and useful platform which helps you cook.
Recipe Roulette enables you to use up the bits and bobs left your cupboard and create tasty dishes with them instead!
